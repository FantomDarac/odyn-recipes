Lasagna


Lasagna originated in Italy during the Middle Ages.
The oldest transcribed text about lasagna appears in 1282 in the Memoriali Bolognesi ("Bolognesi Memorials"),
in which lasagna was mentioned in a poem transcribed by a Bolognese notary; while the first recorded recipe
was set down in the early 14th-century Liber de Coquina (The Book of Cookery).

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Species
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps for preparation

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
